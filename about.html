About Us

Lead Overseas Consultancy

Lead Overseas Consultancy is a registered company registered with Islamic Republic of Afghanistan under the license # 39236, based in Kabul, Afghanistan, It’s an international student recruitment agency that was founded to assist those looking to study in Malaysia, China, Turkey, Ukraine, India, Russia and Europe. Established in early 2017, the organization is being run by a team of well-qualified and experienced professionals with vast Knowledge of student recruitment process. Thanks to it's collaboration with a number of highly-ranked universities and colleges in Malaysia, Turkey, India, and Ukraine, LOC aims to provide the best services in student requirement.

Our Mission

Our Mission is to achieve the reputation of a quality, high standard & reliable solution & service and support our clients through their current and future studies. Our activities, aimed at finding a balance in the education sector serve to give our clients significance, which will support their academic pursuit via the growth of the students and improve their quality of education and learning.

Our Vision

Our Vision is to achieve 100% customer satisfaction by delivering quality services and become an entity in education based corporate solutions. We undertake student recruitment as a mission by placing individuals as the most important element in the process. This is why we created a team that is fully committed to serve our clients in the best possible ways. We believe that client satisfaction is the key to long-term success.
